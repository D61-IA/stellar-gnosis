The easiest, most intuitive, and powerful online tool for managing your journal club. Create public or private journal clubs, set time and place of meeting, allow members to propose papers, and schedule papers for discussion. All these features are just a few clicks away!

Take advantage of our large research paper database indexing all papers from arXiv.org and several other open source conferences and journals. And if a paper is missing, then you can add it with just a couple of clicks.

Organize local and global journal clubs with no restriction on who can join. Gnosis is open to everyone including academics, industry professionals, and citizen scientists who want to keep up with the state-of-the-art in research.
